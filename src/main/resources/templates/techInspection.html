2020 CCSCC Tech Inspection Checklist

Exterior

No Play In Wheel Bearings (Grab tires and wiggle them hard)
Wheels are tight and have no visible cracks
Hubcaps or center caps removed or firmly secured by lug nuts (if applicable)
Tires must be in good condition with no cords/belts showing or cracks.
Tire tread depth (as appropriate)
Brake pads/rotors in good condition
No loose body panels.
Numbers on & clearly visible on body (no blue tape on a blue car & not window)

Interior

Floor mats and all other loose items removed from car
Brake, throttle, and clutch (if applicable) pedals secure
Firm brake pedal
No excessive play in the steering
No excessive play in gear selector
Seat in functional order
Seat Belt proper for year of car and class
Cameras, phones, and other gear mounted to car

Under the Hood and Trunk

Battery and connections secure
Air intake or air box secure
Throttle cable secure (if applicable)
All fluid caps secure
No major leaks apparent
Trunk is empty of items that move around
Functional exhaust system thats not excessively loud and exit behind driver
